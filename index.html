Welcome to Noritani

Noritani is an innovative agribusiness company dedicated to creating healthy, sustainable, and delicious products. We specialize in producing nori made from vegetables, combining traditional techniques with modern agricultural innovation. Our mission is to bring the taste of nature to your table while supporting local farmers and promoting eco-friendly farming practices.

Our Profile

Founded in 2023, Noritani started with a simple idea — transforming local spinach into nutritious, high-quality seaweed alternatives. Our team of agricultural experts and food technologists work hand in hand to ensure every sheet of Noritani Spinach Nori meets international standards of taste, texture, and nutrition.

We believe in empowering Indonesian farmers through sustainable sourcing and fair partnerships. By integrating technology with local wisdom, Noritani continues to grow as a symbol of innovation in the agribusiness sector.
